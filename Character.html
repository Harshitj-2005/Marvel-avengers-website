AVENGERS & ENEMIES

Steve Rogers, as Captain America, embodies courage and leadership with his unbreakable shield.
Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk, a green behemoth of rage and unstoppable strength.
Wade Wilson, the mercenary Deadpool, breaks the fourth wall with humor and deadly skill.
Tony Stark, genius inventor and billionaire, becomes Iron Man to fight for justice and peace.
Thanos, the Mad Titan, seeks ultimate power with the Infinity Stones to reshape the universe.
Matt Murdock, the Daredevil, uses his heightened senses to fight crime in Hell's Kitchen.
Logan, also known as Wolverine, is a fierce mutant with adamantium claws and healing powers.
Wanda Maximoff, the Scarlet Witch, wields chaos magic with incredible power and complexity.
Nick Fury, the mastermind behind the Avengers, is always prepared to tackle any global threat.
Cyclops, the leader of the X-Men, projects powerful optic blasts and guides his team with precision.
Clint Barton, the master archer Hawkeye, takes precise aim to protect the world.
Peter Parker, the friendly neighborhood Spider-Man, balances crime-fighting with being a teenager.
Thor, the God of Thunder, wields his mighty hammer Mjolnir to protect Asgard and Earth.
T'Challa, the Black Panther, defends Wakanda with his unmatched strength and advanced technology.
Natasha Romanoff, the Black Widow, is a highly skilled spy and a key member of the Avengers.
Stephen Strange, the Sorcerer Supreme, defends the Earth against mystical threats.
Jennifer Walters, the She-Hulk, combines her legal expertise with superhuman strength.
Miles Morales, a young Spider-Man, brings fresh energy and unique powers to the mantle.
Nova, the human rocket, defends the galaxy with his cosmic powers and bravery.
Scott Lang, the Ant-Man, uses shrinking technology to tackle big problems in small ways.
