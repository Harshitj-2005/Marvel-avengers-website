LATEST MOVIES

Avengers: Infinity War is a 2018 superhero film that brings together the Avengers and the Guardians of the Galaxy to confront the formidable Thanos, who seeks to collect all six Infinity Stones to impose his will on the universe. The film is the 19th installment in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and serves as a direct sequel to "Avengers: Age of Ultron" (2015).
The Marvels (2023) is a superhero film from Marvel Studios, uniting Captain Marvel, Monica Rambeau, and Ms. Marvel as they confront a cosmic threat while their powers become mysteriously entangled. The film explores teamwork, identity, and the balance of responsibility in the MCU.
Guardians of the Galaxy (2014) introduces a misfit team of intergalactic outlaws who band together to stop a cosmic threat while finding family in each other. Led by Star-Lord, the group faces off against powerful villains, with humor and heart defining their adventures.
Captain Marvel (2019) is an origin story about Carol Danvers, a former Air Force pilot who becomes one of the universe's most powerful heroes after gaining cosmic powers. Set in the 1990s, the film explores her journey of self-discovery and her role in the Kree-Skrull conflict.
Avengers: Endgame (2019) follows the surviving Avengers as they attempt to reverse the catastrophic events caused by Thanos in Infinity War by traveling through time to collect the Infinity Stones. The film culminates in an epic battle to save the universe, with key sacrifices that close out the Infinity Saga.
The upcoming Avengers: Doomsday (2026) will bring together a new generation of heroes, with major roles for characters like Doctor Doom, Shang-Chi, the new Black Panther, and others. Following the multiverse saga, this film will explore conflicts tied to the vast, multiversal threats, possibly addressing unresolved storylines from Loki and Ant-Man: Quantumania, while potentially incorporating the Young Avengers
Black Widow (2021) explores Natasha Romanoff's past as she confronts the shadows of her former life as a spy, dealing with unresolved issues from her time with the Red Room. Set after the events of Captain America: Civil War, the film delves into her relationship with her "family" and the fight against a new threat, Taskmaster.
Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings (2021) introduces Shang-Chi, a skilled martial artist who must confront his past and his powerful father, Wenwu, the leader of the Ten Rings organization. The film explores themes of identity, family, and destiny as Shang-Chi discovers the true power of the mystical Ten Rings and takes on a larger role in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
